# Prior Art Checklist

Before public claims, compare against:

- FLAME and associated fluorescence datasets.
- ChemFluor.
- FluoDB-like large fluorophore datasets.
- Fluor-tools.
- Deep4Chem.
- Sensight and related imaging-probe optimization frameworks.
- General molecular property benchmarks.
- Analytical chemistry sensor databases.
- Electrochemical sensor ML resources.

## Safe claim

SensorGenome focuses on condition-standardized molecular sensor response and active-learning experiment selection.

## Unsafe claim

Do not claim to be the first fluorescence ML dataset or first fluorophore prediction platform.
